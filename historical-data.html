Exchange Rate Comparison Tool





Our Historical Exchange Rate Comparison Tool provides you with accurate comparison rates of various currencies on a specific date of your choosing. Our comprehensive data spans back approximately two years, guaranteeing its reliability.

To utilize the tool, simply input the base currency, target currency, and your desired date. Upon submitting your request, a pop-up window above will display the relevant exchange rate information. Feel free to explore and benefit from this valuable resource.

NOTE: In the context of our tool,

  • the Base Currency refers to the currency you are converting from,
  • while the Target Currency represents the currency you wish to convert to.
It's important to specify both currencies accurately to obtain the desired exchange rate comparison.
